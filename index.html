NeuroFlow: Toward Unified Visual Encoding and Decoding from Neural Activity

Abstract

Understanding the neural mechanisms of visual encoding and decoding is a core research category in neuroscience and critical for advancing brain-computer interfaces (BCIs). We propose NeuroFlow, the first unified model for visual encoding and decoding directly from neural activity, providing a solid foundation for building bidirectional visual BCIs.

Overview

NeuroFlow unifies visual encoding and decoding via cross-modal flow matching, enabling consistent and accurate mapping between neural signals and visual content.
